["Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine, the largest mobilization of forces in Europe since 1945, evokes Cold War anxieties. Despite facing fierce Ukrainian resistance and a less-than-swift victory, Putin aims to capture Kyiv, overthrow the government, and bring Ukraine back into Russia's sphere of influence. The invasion threatens regional stability, Europe's security structure, and has prompted Putin to try and diminish NATO. Frustrated by the slow advance, Russia is intensifying attacks on civilian targets. The war has caused a devastating humanitarian crisis, with thousands of deaths and the fastest-growing refugee crisis since World War II. Diplomatic talks have failed, leading to severe economic sanctions and the withdrawal of Western businesses from Russia."]
