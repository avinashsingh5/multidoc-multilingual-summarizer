["Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine, the largest military mobilization in Europe since 1945, has destabilized the region and created a major humanitarian crisis. Despite facing strong Ukrainian resistance and international sanctions, Putin aims to capture Kyiv, overthrow the government, and bring Ukraine back into Russia's sphere of influence. The invasion, marked by intensified attacks on civilian targets, has resulted in thousands of deaths and a massive refugee crisis. Diplomatic efforts have so far failed to halt the war, and the West has responded with severe economic sanctions and the withdrawal of many businesses from Russia."]
